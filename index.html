About us

Welcome to Guadalupe's Mexican Restaurant, where tradition meets flavor! Established in 1990, we’ve been proudly serving the Gladewater community with authentic Tex-Mex cuisine for over three decades.

Our story began with a simple mission: to bring the vibrant tastes of Mexico and Texas to your table, using fresh ingredients, time-honored recipes, and a whole lot of love.

At Guadalupe's, every dish tells a story. From our sizzling fajitas to our mouthwatering enchiladas, we craft each meal with care and passion. Our secret? A family recipe book passed down through generations, combined with a commitment to quality and hospitality. Located in the heart of Gladewater, Texas, we’re more than just a restaurant—we’re a gathering place for families, friends, and food lovers. Whether you’re joining us for a quick lunch, a festive dinner, or a special celebration, we invite you to experience the warmth and flavor that make Guadalupe's a local favorite. Thank you for being part of our journey. We look forward to serving you and sharing the taste of tradition, one plate at a time.
